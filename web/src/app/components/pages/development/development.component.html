BEGINNING STAGES

Originally we wanted to have an art installation that just dealt with the topic of body image and loving yourself. A big part of loving yourself is accepting who you are and being comfortable in your own skin. The media today has set a standard for unhealthy and even unobtainable standards about beauty. The Dove Self Esteem project has done many unique projects inspired by this very same topic. We thought that it would be highly relevant and beneficial to bring something like that to our campus.

The idea was to set up a mirror in a high traffic area on campus. We wanted to provide people with an opportunity to see themselves in the mirrior and write something positive or encouraging about themselves or other people. We hoped that this to build up over the week and that it would be something that people wanted to participate in.

To create daily interest over the week, we wanted to have elements that remained everyday and elements that changed everyday. We wanted to have a large board where we could post and change content relevant to each day’s theme and topics we wanted to discuss.

We didn’t use this idea because we couldn’t book the library grass or acquire such a board.
