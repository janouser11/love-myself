Our installation was located right outside of the library. Each day we asked people to respond to a different prompt. We handed out our own personal merchandise as well as items from the organizations that we partnered with. Each day we reached over 100 people. We had several people who returned each day.

Day 1: Write something you like about yourself. Green slips. Handed out stickers.
Day 2: Write a compliment you wished you told someone but didn’t. Pink slips. Handed out buttons.
Day 3 Write out one of your goals or dreams​. Yellow slips. Handed out candy.
Day 4 Write something to encourage yourself. Blue slips. Handed out cards.
Day 5 Write one way that you express yourself. Purple Slips. Handed out paper flowers.
