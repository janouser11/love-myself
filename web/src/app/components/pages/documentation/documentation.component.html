Our installation was located right outside of the library. Each day we asked people to respond to a different prompt. We handed out our own personal merchandise as well as items from the organizations that we partnered with. Each day we reached over 100 people, several of whom returned each day. We handed out an assortment of deliverables given to us by our partners as well as our own personal merchandise.

Day 1: Green. Accepting yourself. Write something you like about yourself.
Day 2: Pink. Forgiving yourself. Write a compliment you wished you told someone but didn’t.
Day 3 Yellow. Believing in yourself. Write out one of your goals or dreams​.
Day 4 Blue. Encouraging yourself. Write something to encourage yourself.
Day 5 Purple. Expressing yourself. Write one way that you express yourself.
